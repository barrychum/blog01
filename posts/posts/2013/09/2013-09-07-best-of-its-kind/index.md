The followings are some of my favorite 'best of its kind' open source software (or has a usable free version)

**Router** vyatta

**Encryption (Windows)** Truecrypt

**Cloud backup (Windows)** Duplicati

**Cloud synchronization (Windows)** Allway Sync

**Backup (Windows)** areca backup

**Diskcloning (Windows)** Paragon Reflect

**Disk analysis** SpaceSniffer

**Cross platform development** Phonegap

**Search engine** Zoom Search Engine

**Keyboard status** Traystatus

**Key mapping** Keytweak

**Screen capture**

greenshot screenshot captor

**Windows start menu** (for Win8) classic menu
